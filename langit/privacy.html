Your information

We do not collect, or process your personal, or sensitive information.

Your logged-in Bluesky accounts, and any user interface configuration are stored in the browser local storage.

If configured, backups of your user interface configuration may be stored in your PDS' account preferences storage. No other preferences are touched in the process, and any preferences stored there are bound by your respective server's privacy policy.

Third-party services

We make use of Cloudflare's content delivery network to serve our website to you. However, their privacy policy do not apply to visiting our website. If you want to read it anyway, their policy can be read here.
